QR Toolkit Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Overview

QR Toolkit is an offline QR scanner and generator app. It processes QR codes locally on the user's device.

Data Collection

QR Toolkit does not collect personal data. The app does not transmit scanned QR data, generated QR data, contact/vCard data, images, screenshots, or history records to any server controlled by the developer.

QR Toolkit does not sell, rent, or share user information.

Camera Permission

Camera permission is used only for live QR scanning. Camera frames are processed locally on the device for QR recognition.

Gallery and Image Access

Gallery or image access is used only when the user selects an image or screenshot for QR scanning. Selected images are processed locally on the device.

History

Scan and generation history is stored locally on the device. Users can clear history from inside the app.

Contact and vCard Data

Contact/vCard data is processed locally only. When the user chooses to add a contact, QR Toolkit uses Android system intents to open the device's contact flow.

External Actions

Opening links, email addresses, phone numbers, or contacts uses Android system intents. Those actions may open other apps or websites chosen by the user's device.

Ads, Analytics, Login, and Servers

QR Toolkit does not include ads, analytics, account creation, login, or a developer-operated server for QR processing.

Contact

For privacy questions, provide the support contact configured in the Google Play Console listing.
